About Us

Entertainment Ready is a entertainment company specializing in dating workshops/events for Philadelphia singles, focusing on safety and fun. We have coined this style of dating, “PG Dating” as our workshops and events are meant to PREPARE and GUIDE our singles in the dating world. We focus not on match-making, rather, the empowerment of our singles by using self discovery and teaching self worth. We blend dating education and fun interactive games tailored to the modern day bachelor and bachelorette..

By taking the face-to-face dating experience in combination with fun interactive games to stimulate real chemistry, we have been able to create the best recipe for a lifetime of success stories.
